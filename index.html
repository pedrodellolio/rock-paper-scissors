Rules


Welcome to the Rock-Paper-Scissors Simulation!


In this simulation, entities can move around the canvas. When they collide, they interact following the rules of the Rock-Paper-Scissors game:


  • Rock loses to Paper
  • Paper loses to Scissors
  • Scissors lose to Rock

Feel free to experiment with different number of entities, but keep in mind that for a balanced and fair simulation, using equal values for all entities is ideal.


Have fun and see which entity type emerges as the winner!
